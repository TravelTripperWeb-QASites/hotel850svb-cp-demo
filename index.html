THE BEST OF BOTH WORLDS. YOURS AND OURS.

Located at the intersection of West Hollywood and Beverly Hills, Hotel 850 is a new destination from hotelier Jeff Klein that blends the comfort and ease of residential living with the design and service of a luxury boutique hotel.
